Frequently asked questions

Straight answers about building custom software with us.

How much does custom software cost?

Custom projects start from $1,999. The final price depends on scope and features — after we understand what you need, you get a clear fixed quote up front, billed in USD with SAR invoicing available for Saudi businesses.

How long does it take to build?

A small tool can take a couple of weeks; a full business system usually takes one to two months. Your proposal includes a realistic timeline before any work begins.

Do I own the software?

Yes. You get a perpetual licence to use the software for your business, your data stays on your own PC, and there is no forced subscription.

Can you customise or extend software I already have?

If we built it, absolutely. For other software we can usually add features, build reports, or rebuild it to work the way you need — tell us what you have and what you want.

What about updates and support?

The first year of support is included. After that, ongoing support and updates are optional and quoted per project — you are never locked in.

Do you work with businesses remotely?

Yes. We work with businesses in Saudi Arabia and beyond. Discovery, demos, delivery and support are all handled remotely.
