What Clutch actually does

Every feature either fixes something real or tells you the honest truth about your lag.

Bandwidth firewall

During a match, the background apps you pick lose internet — so all your bandwidth goes to the game. Runs 100% on your phone.

Thermal watch

Warns you before your phone overheats and throttles your FPS — using the real Android thermal status, not a guess.

Focus mode

Silences notifications and full-screen call take-overs mid-match with Do Not Disturb, so nothing yanks you out of a fight.

Floating panel

Live smoothness, temperature, RAM and network — right over the game, with quick toggles and a gesture lock.

Network Doctor

Real ping, jitter and packet-loss to your router and the internet — so you can see whether it's your Wi-Fi or your ISP.

Device report

Reads your chipset and RAM and gives an honest settings guide for Free Fire — no game files changed, ever.

Honest by design

Most boosters lie to look busy. Clutch refuses to — here's exactly where we draw the line.

What we will never do

  • No fake FPS boost, no fake "RAM cleaner", no fake ping reducer — placebos that waste battery and do nothing.
  • We never modify, read or touch Free Fire (or any game). Working only from the outside keeps your account safe.
  • The smoothness meter is shown as an estimate — true per-game FPS needs root, and we won't pretend otherwise.
  • Ping depends on your ISP and route. Clutch reduces lag-spikes and keeps your line clear; it does not promise a magic number.
  • 100% on-device. No accounts, no tracking, no data collected, no uploads. The local VPN blocks background apps only — it never routes or inspects your traffic.
