One ledger, no more registers

Every trip entry is validated as you type — one source of truth for every balance.

  • Master orders split freight across sub-trips with properly allocated costs.
  • Edit or void any entry — always with a trail.
  • Archive old periods and keep the working ledger clean.

Billing your clients trust

Keep a rate file per client and turn quotations into invoices in seconds.

  • Every generated invoice lands in Invoice History with its period.
  • Each invoice carries a unique verification code.
  • Any printed invoice can be checked later against the record.

A full manpower module

If you supply workers, the ledger covers that too — wages on one side, company billing on the other.

  • Attendance in bulk, advances and expense categories.
  • Outstanding computed automatically, All-Clear settlement with undo.
  • Worker statement, summary and billing reports in Excel and PDF.

Frequently asked questions

Straight answers about how Transport Ledger works.

Is my data stored online?

No. Transport Ledger is a Windows desktop app. Your data lives in your own PC, and backups go where you choose — nothing is uploaded anywhere.

What do I need to run it?

A normal Windows 10 or 11 computer. No server, no internet connection and no monthly hosting.

Can I use it on more than one PC?

Each license is locked to one PC. If you need more seats, contact us and we will set you up.

Can I move everything to a new computer?

Yes. Full export/import transfer is built in — your complete data moves over, and we issue a license for the new machine.

Can features be customised for my business?

Yes. We build and maintain Transport Ledger ourselves, so tailored features and reports are possible — tell us what your operation needs.

How do I get a demo and pricing?

Email us at hello@xniperbuilds.com. We will show you the software live and quote per business — no commitment.
