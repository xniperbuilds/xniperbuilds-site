Frequently asked questions

Straight answers about how Rent Ledger works.

Does it fit a Saudi rent-a-car business?

Yes. Rent Ledger uses SAR currency, Saudi mobile formats for WhatsApp notices, and a clean English interface — it was built for exactly this trade.

Is my data stored online?

No. It is a Windows desktop app; your data stays in your own PC and your backups go where you choose.

Will partner details show on printed sheets?

No names, ever. Partners appear only as a colour on serial cells and dots inside the app — discreet by design.

Can I move everything to a new computer?

Yes. Built-in transfer moves your complete data, and we issue a license for the new machine.

Can features be customised?

Yes. We build and maintain Rent Ledger ourselves — reports and features can be tailored to how your office works.
