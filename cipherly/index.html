Privacy you can hold

Strong encryption wrapped in things that are actually fun to use — all on-device.

AES-256 lock

Password-lock any text with AES-256-GCM. Only the right password opens it.

Turn secrets into innocent emojis, or hide them in invisible ink no one can see.
